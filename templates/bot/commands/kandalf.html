COMMANDS
Kandalf

Moderation
.ban [user] (reason) - Bans a user
.kick [user] (reason) - Kicks a user
.unban [user] - Unbans a user
.clear [amount] - Clears a specific amount of text

Other tools
.embed - lets you create a nice embed
.kandalf - Gives you more information about the bot
.ping - Shows the latency of the bot
.status - Lets you change the status of the bot!

Music
.join - Makes the bot join a channel you are in
.leave - Makes the bot leave a channel its currently in
.play [search or url] - lets you play a song from youtube
.volume [volume %] - Changes the volume
.pause - Pauses a currently played song
.resume - Resumes a currently paused song
.stop - Stops a currently played song
